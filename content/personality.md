## Personality

Rasmus is very passionate about his role as a developer. He always
strives to produce the best possible results and enjoys problem
solving. Solving tough problems is something that gives Rasmus a
lot of joy and is one of the reasons he loves to work with
programming.

Rasmus believes that seeing the whole picture, thinking and planning
well are traits that symbolize a good developer. Being able to cooperate and
help each other is also traits that is most important for a team to succeed.

On Rasmus free time he enjoys spending time with his family, programming
and learning.
